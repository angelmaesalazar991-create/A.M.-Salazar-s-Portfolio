The Best Movies of all time

My top 5 favorite movies.

Elemental

It has a cool idea of using fire, water, earth, and air as characters. It talks about important things like belonging and being yourself, which many people can relate to.

Toy Story 4

The animation is stunning and full of colorful details. The movie has funny moments and also makes you feel emotional, especially about friendship and letting go.

Inside Out 2

This movie dives deeper into emotions, showing how they grow and change as we get older. It balances humor with important lessons about growing up and understanding ourselves.

Rio 2

The movie has catchy music and lively dance numbers that are enjoyable to watch. It also emphasizes family, friendship, and the importance of protecting nature.

Zootopia 2

The movie dicusses important themes like teamwork and acceptance in a way that everyone can understand. It has humor, action, and heart warming moments that make it enjoyable for all ages.
